What is HackTKS?

HackTKS is a highschool Club @The Kalyani School, Pune we encourage highschool students from TKS to come and lean Programming! There will be code, There will be stickers, and most of all there will be lots of fun! Best of all it's entirely free!

Who are we?

We are a group of high school students from Pune who were frustrated by the lack of high school level programming events and clubs in our school, so we created our own! Our goal with HackTKS is to bring together students to form cross-campus connections, learn from each other, and build on each others' skillsets with real world experience.

Details!

When: Every Month End (except before exams)
Where: The Kalyani School
Who can register: Any highschool student from TKS
Why: It's fun!
